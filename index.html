A Little Bit About Me:

I'm a mechanical engineer and an artist at heart.

There's a certain spark that engineering ignites when an idea becomes reality. I stumbled into engineering in college not really knowing anything about it. what I thought was just a math-based art field turned into so much more. I've been lucky enough to be a part of some incredible projects that will impact many lives.

Art is a lot like engineering. There is a precision yet freedom in the formation of art. I've befriended my camera and committed to capturing moving images and videos. Follow my journey at OneHundred Photos, coming soon.

Outside of engineeing and art, I love playing and watching sports (football, basketball, and rock climbing), watching shows and movies, hanging out with friends and family, and trying new things.
